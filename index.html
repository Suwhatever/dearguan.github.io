How the Buyer Score Works

▼

The engine ranks listings using a buyer-oriented score built from five practical components. It is mainly a good-value mainstream-home finder, not a luxury ranking. The strongest recommendations are listings that look materially cheaper per m² than similar homes in the same area and property type.
